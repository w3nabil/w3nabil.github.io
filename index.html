# ABOUT NABIL

                                    

                                        
Nabil Islam (also known as w3nabil, born
                                            2006) is a dedicated high school graduate with a strong passion for the
                                            ethical hacking. Starting at the age of 15, Nabil gained valuable experience
                                            working with several prestigious online game communities, including Hyper
                                            Front, Dead by Daylight Mobile (Netease), and NEW STATE MOBILE, among
                                            others. This marked the beginning of his career in community management,
                                            where he started as a part-time professional.

                                        
In 2022, Nabil paused his professional journey to
                                            focus on his secondary school examinations. During this time, he committed
                                            himself to academic excellence and achieved outstanding results, obtaining a
                                            GPA of 5.00 out of 5.00 in both his secondary and higher secondary exams.
                                        

                                        
Following his higher secondary exams, Nabil faced
                                            significant financial challenges that threatened his ability to pursue
                                            university education.
